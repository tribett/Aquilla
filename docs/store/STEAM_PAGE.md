# Aquilla — Store Page Draft

## Short Description
A Christian fantasy adventure in the spirit of classic top-down epics. Guide Aquilla and his sheepdog Bracken across five chapters — gather the lost, guard the weak, and restore what mercy can heal.

## About This Game
**Aquilla** is a handcrafted pixel-art adventure spanning Briarfold Valley, the Ashen Moor, the High Kingsroad, and the fallen courts of Elarion. Explore room-by-room, calm corrupted guardians without violence, solve shrine puzzles, play the Harp of Remembrance, and break the Lucent Court's false light.

### Features
- **5-chapter epic** — ~4–6 hours full clear, 8–10 with side quests
- **Mercy-first encounters** — distract, steady, and restore rather than defeat
- **Sheepdog companion** — herd, fetch, stay, warn, and track scent trails
- **18 optional side quests** — valley tasks and kingdom secrets
- **6 achievements** — from Fold restored to Crown Witness
- **Chapter themes in Web Audio** — procedural tones and restore motifs
- **Autosave** — continue Aquilla's witness across sessions
